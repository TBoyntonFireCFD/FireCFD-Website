How We Help You Achieve Fire Safety

BS 7974

We specialise in providing clients with performance-based Fire Engineered Solutions through the BS 7974 framework using the NIST Fire Dynamics Simulator.

Design Fires

We determine the complex and interconnected characteristics of reasonable worst-case fires bespoke for your project, and describe these characteristics quantitatively in our model.

Scientific Clarity

We approach every project with total clarity on the research, academic reference, and real-world data which informs our decisions and calculations.

ASET & RSET

We find both the time occupants need to evacuate and the time available for safe escape before conditions become untenable due to smoke and fire.
